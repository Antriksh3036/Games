This game has 3 elements --> 1.Snake(s) ,2.Water(w) ,3.Gun(g)
The computer will choose one of these and you will choose one of these
If Snake vs Water -> Snake wins , Snake vs Gun -> Gun wins , Water vs Gun -> Water wins
There will be 10 rounds and who wins the maximum rounds , wins the game
If a round is draw then no one gets point
If the game ends in a draw , a super-round will be held and the winner will be decided based on that super-round
